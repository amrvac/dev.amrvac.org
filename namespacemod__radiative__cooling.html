Detailed Description

module radiative cooling – add optically thin radiative cooling

only uses the (Townsend) exact integration method, can be used in HD, ffhd, MHD, twofl

Cooling curves assume an H/He plasma and ionization equilibrium. The gas temperature is supplied by the active physics EOS; the cooling table composition and abundance assumptions remain curve-dependent. Formula: Q=-n_H*n_e*f(T), where f(T) is tabulated or piecewise power law.
