Detailed Description

module containing all optically thin radiative cooling tables

Used by mod_radiative_cooling.t for HD and ffhd and MHD and twofl Assumptions: full ionized plasma dominated by H and He, ionization equilibrium Formula: Q=-n_H*n_e*f(T), positive f(T) function is pre-computed and tabulated or a piecewise power law
